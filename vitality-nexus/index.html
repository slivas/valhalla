Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about the Vitality Nexus Hub experience.

What is the Vitality Nexus Hub?

The Vitality Nexus Hub is your centralized dashboard for all Valhalla Vitality services. It brings together AI-powered assistants to support your wellness journey through personalized coaching, 24/7 support, and secure medical communication.

How do the AI assistants work together?

Each AI assistant has a specific role: Thoryn provides wellness coaching and plans, Astrid offers general support and navigation help, and the Medical Question Bot facilitates secure communication with your care team. Your responses to medical questions help improve the recommendations from Thoryn and Astrid.

Is my medical information secure?

Yes, absolutely. All medical communications go through our secure Medical Question Bot, which is compliant with healthcare privacy regulations. Your care team uses this secure channel to ask questions and receive your responses safely.

How often should I check the dashboard?

We recommend checking your dashboard daily to see any new medical questions, wellness plan updates, or messages from your AI assistants. You'll see notification badges when there's something new that needs your attention.

Can I customize my experience?

The interface is designed to provide a consistent, optimal experience for all users. While you can't customize themes or layouts, your content is automatically personalized based on your health profile and responses.
