Eligibility & Safety

Common questions about our GLP-1 care program and eligibility requirements.

Who's eligible for GLP-1 therapy?

Eligibility depends on your medical history and clinician assessment; it isn't right for everyone. Start by completing the intake to learn if you qualify.

Will I see specific medications or prices on this page?

No. To reduce "hard advertising," we show detailed options and pricing only to patients inside the portal. Complete the intake to unlock access.

What is GLP-1 vs GLP-1+?

Your clinician may consider GLP-1 or GLP-1+ options based on your needs; choices are made case-by-case after intake. (Generic framing—no brand names on LP.)

Insurance & affordability?

Coverage varies. Your care team will advise options (including compounded or alternatives when appropriate) after eligibility.

Side effects and monitoring?

Your provider will review common effects and set a follow-up cadence tailored to you.

GLP-1 options are available to patients within our clinical network. This page provides information only. We don't influence prescribing decisions—those are made solely by your clinician based on medical necessity.
